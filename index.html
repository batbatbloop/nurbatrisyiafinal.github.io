Hidden Beauty: Pang Sua Pond

Pang Sua Pond at Bukit Panjang

If you're not from the North-West area of Singapore, I bet you did not know about the small but charming Pang Sua Pond. Don't underestimate its beauty because of its small size. Like a little chilli padi, it can surprise you how much you can see and do there. Part of the ABC Waters project by the PUB, Pang Sua Pond is certainly a beautiful, clean place you can keep active at. It is stunning any time of the day with its newly developed boardwalk and floating wetlands. Against the backdrop of the Bukit Panjang HDB blocks, the view here encapsulates Singapore's garden-city concept perfectly. The floating wetlands also help to improve the water quality of the pond. The best part is that it is conveniently located near many other ameneities (so you don't have to worry about toilets).

Watch this YouTube video for a nice tour of the pond and the surrounding park.

There's so much to see and do here throughout the day for a simple outing.
