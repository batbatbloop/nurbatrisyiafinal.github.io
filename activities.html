Pang Sua Pond: Night View

At night, the lights on the boardwalk sets a romantic mood for a slow walk under the moonlight. There are even spots along the boardwalk where small pavillion-like shelters and seats are available for a small late-night picnic for couples or friends. Also, since Bukit Panjang Plaza is literally across the road, you don't have to go far to get your picnic snacks. Alternatively, there's a bubbletea shop at the Community Centre right next to the pond.

Sometimes, there are events held at the stage by the pond (as seen in the video) which you can watch for free! The community centre sometimes holds movie nights or karaoke nights there.
